Pricing FAQ

Everything you need to know before not being able to buy anything.

Is Is It Vibe Coded free?

Yes. The Hobby plan is free forever and includes three forensic scans per lunar cycle. Pro and Enterprise add unlimited scans and considerably more theatre.

Can I actually buy a plan?

No — and that is the joke. Is It Vibe Coded is a satirical project, so clicking any purchase button returns a cheerful error. Digital licences are perpetually, deliberately out of stock.

What's the difference between the plans?

Mostly wording. Hobby runs the first three pipeline stages; Pro unlocks coffee-stain spectrometry and lunar commit analysis; Enterprise adds a dedicated Chamber of Vibe Analysts and a Raspberry Pi you will never receive.

Do you offer refunds?

The vibes are strictly non-refundable. Happily, since nothing can be purchased, this has never once come up.
